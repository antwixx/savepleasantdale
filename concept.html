A Community of Care

Tucked away in unincorporated Lemont, Illinois, Pleasantdale is more than just a subdivision; it is a sanctuary. Since 1957, our 320 single-family homes have been a place where neighbors know each other by name, where retirees watch over young families, and where children ride their bikes safely down quiet, tree-lined streets.

We are a community built on history and connection. But today, that peace is under threat.

The Threat to Our Health & Home

A proposed large-scale Gas N Wash truck stop is targeting our borders. This development threatens to bring massive commercial traffic, underground fuel storage, and noise pollution directly to our residential doorsteps.

Environment

Risk of groundwater and air pollution affecting the adjacent Waterfall Glen Forest Preserve.

Safety

Heavy truck traffic on local roads endangering our children and pedestrians.

Future

Irreversible damage to the character and property values of a historic neighborhood.
