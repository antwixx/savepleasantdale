URGENT: Village Ordinance Passed

The Village of Lemont passed an ordinance on August 11th, 2025 to annex four unincorporated properties on Lemont Road. These properties are part of the residential Pleasantdale neighborhood.

These properties are now incorporated into Lemont and fall under Lemont's jurisdiction. The next step would be for the owner to request Lemont to re-zone the properties from residential to commercial.
