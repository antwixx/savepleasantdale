Information & Details

Understanding the truck stop proposal and its potential impact on our community.

Development Timeline

Village Ordinance Passed

August 11, 2025

Village of Lemont passed ordinance to annex four unincorporated homes on Lemont Road.
The Official Annexation Ordinance can be viewed here: Lemont Annexation Ordinance

Properties Incorporated

Current Status

These four properties now fall under Lemont's jurisdiction as part of the Pleasantdale neighborhood

Potential Rezoning Request URGENT

Next Step

Property owner may request rezoning from residential to commercial for gas station development

Impact Overview

Traffic Impact

Many large commercial vehicles daily through residential streets

  • 24/7 truck traffic on Lemont Road
  • Damage to residential road infrastructure
  • Increased congestion and safety risks
  • Noise from diesel engines and air brakes

Wells & Septic

Drinking water and septic systems at risk of contamination

  • Contamination risks to private wells
  • No routine testing for homeowner water safety
  • Septic systems disrupted by paving and runoff
  • Dual threats from fuel leaks and septic discharge

Environmental Concerns

Air and noise pollution affecting community health

  • Diesel emissions and air quality degradation
  • Potential groundwater contamination
  • 24/7 noise pollution from trucks and operations
  • Light pollution from 24-hour operations
