Communication Tips

Be Specific

Reference specific properties, dates, and ordinances to show you're informed

Stay Professional

Maintain a respectful tone while being firm about your concerns

Show Community Support

Mention that you represent broader neighborhood concerns, not just personal interests

Act Quickly

Time is critical - send your messages promptly and encourage others to do the same
